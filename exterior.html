Our 20 Step Process for Exterior Painting

For each project 360° Painting Fort Lauderdale tackles, our painters implement our 20 Step Process, guaranteed to deliver a customized quality product. The process carries each exterior painting project we undertake from beginning to end, mapping out the best plan of action of action, executing with the best approach, and spot checking our work at project’s end. No 360° Painting job is complete until we conduct a final walk through with each home owner we serve to guarantee we have finished the work to your absolute satisfaction. We pride ourselves on amazing you with our results.

The 360° Painting 20 Step Process is simple, but thorough. After you have selected your preference in paint color and finish type, we will prep your home’s exterior accordingly for the painting work to come. We will move all outdoor furniture, grills, and plants to protect all of your belongings. We will power wash the home’s surface, followed by scraping and feather sanding it to ensure all of the old paint is removed.

For each area of the home’s surface, we will use the most appropriate painting method. Our painters are proficient in various approaches, including rollers, brushes, and spray techniques. After applying the necessary number of coats for a superior finish, we complete any final spot touches and review our work to guarantee we have complete your home’s exterior painting job at the highest possible level.
